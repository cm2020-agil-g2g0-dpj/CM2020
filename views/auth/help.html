About the APP

The Product Lifecycle Management App is all about helping you manage your product lifecycle more effeciently. The application allows you to keep track of your workflow and status of design across tools and processes. The Project Lifecycle Management Linker application allows product modeling to be combined with part-specific data along with product management and communication functionalities.


How it works

The application allow you to create individual parts of your solution is an central repository

The parts can then be linked to each to other to form a complete solution, with the benefit being all the parts forming a complete solution can be mananaged in a central space.
